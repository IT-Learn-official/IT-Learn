About Us

Discover our journey from idea to reality.

🎉 June 2025 (Idea)

Broodje56 and Joren started this project with a simple idea, to create a platform where people can learn and grow together in IT.

📝 June 2025 (Planning)

We brainstormed the main features, sketched a roadmap, and planned the first version of IT Learn.

💻 July 2025 (Prototype)

Built the first prototype of the platform, tested the lesson system, and received initial feedback from friends.

🎨 August 2025 (Redesign)

We redesigned the lessons, landing page, and login system, creating a smoother user experience.

💵 September 2025 (Funding)

We looked for funding to launch the platform while continuing to improve it behind the scenes.

🧪 September 2025 (Beta Test)

Selected users helped us test the platform to gather feedback on usability and content quality.

🤝 October 2025

Broodje56 and Joren finally met in real life. Until then, the website was built entirely via Discord.

🚀 November 2025 (Soft Launch)

We released a soft launch to the first users to test the platform publicly and ensure stability.

📈 Present

We continue to enhance the platform, adding new features, lessons, and opportunities to help learners succeed in IT.
